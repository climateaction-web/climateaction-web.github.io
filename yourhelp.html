How You Can Help

Below Are Some Ways You Can Help To Fight Climate Change

Reduce Your Elecitricy Usage

Reducing your electricity usage will reduce how much Greenhouse gas emissions we create because creating electricity is mostly non-renewable, which means the resources used to create it is, Coal, Oil, And Natural gasses, which are fossil fuels. When we burn fossil fuels, to create energy, we create carbon dioxide, also known as a greenhouse gas, so the more electricity we use, the more gasses we make. So to cut down on our carbon dioxide production, we need to use less electricity.

Drive And Fly Less

Flying and drive less will reduce carbon dioxide production by a lot. Other ways of transport are, Walking or Cycling, Take public transport, Carpool with friends, Go on fewer flights or pay to offset your emissions when you fly. If we do, then we will reduce the carbon dioxide production coming from vehicles.

Plant Trees

Planting Trees will help to reduce the carbon dioxide we have produced, because trees absorb carbon dioxide and other potentially harmful gasses, such as sulfur dioxide and carbon monoxide to produce oxygen. So the more trees we have the more carbon dioxide we will get rid of. Fun Fact: One large tree can supply a day's supply of oxygen for four people.

Eat Less Meat

Red meat production produces quite a bit of Greenhouse gasses, Such as Methane, because cows fart and that and make Methane. Because one of the main animals they use is cows because they make beef, cheese, milk, and leather. It also requires a large amount of water. Did you know: Around 30 percent of the world's land is used for livestock, and it is one of the key reasons for cutting down the forest.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

All products require energy and materials to be made, transported, and sold. By buying only what you need, reusing what you have, and recycling your old products, you will make the factories use less energy to make fewer products, to use less fuel to transport fewer products and for the sellers to sell fewer products. You will make everyone use less energy to make, transport, and sell the products.
